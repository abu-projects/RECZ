RECZ — MASTER FEATURE LIST
 
 
1. ONBOARDING & IDENTITY SETUP
Account Creation & Authentication Standard registration via social login (Google, Apple, Facebook) or email/phone. The entry gate into the app.
Interest & Vibe Onboarding A multi-step flow that captures the user's taste categories (food, movies, travel, nightlife, wellness, etc.), dietary preferences, favorite cuisines, and cultural interests. This is not just signup — it's the first act of building the user's taste identity.
Location & City Selection Captures the user's home city and allows location access for proximity-based discovery. Informs which Recz Insiders, restaurants, and events are surfaced.
Gender & Date of Birth Collection Optional but benefit-framed demographic inputs. Gender informs vibe matching, community spaces, and safety filtering. Date of birth unlocks age-appropriate experiences, birthday features, and lifecycle personalization.
Recz Insiders Introduction New users are introduced to Recz Insiders (AI Buddy) during onboarding so the feed is never empty from day one. Sets expectations for Rexy and the social discovery layer before real social connections are built. Based on the user's persona, location, and interests, Recz auto-follows a curated set of Recz Insiders — the onboarding screen previews which ones will be followed, and the user can opt out of any.
 
2. TASTE IDENTITY (TASTE PROFILE)
Taste Profile Engine The underlying system that continuously builds a multi-dimensional representation of who the user is based on what they post, save, like, and interact with — across food, film, travel, music, books, wellness, and more.
Taste Identity Graphic (Taste Code) A visual, QR-code-inspired spherical graphic unique to each user that encodes their taste profile as a scannable identity artifact. Layer 1 shows category weights (color arcs); Layer 2 encodes depth and specificity within each category. Designed to be shareable, wearable, and scannable between users IRL.
Taste Matching The mechanism by which Recz surfaces compatible users, places, content, and experiences by comparing taste profiles. Works across directions: find people like me, find restaurants that match me, find films that fit me.
 
3. RECOMMENDATION POST (THE CORE CONTENT UNIT)
Rec Post Creation The primary user action on Recz. A user recommends something — a restaurant, film, book, travel destination, product, experience, show, music, recipe, or any other category — with a photo, caption, rating, tags, location, and occasion context.
Category-Specific Post Fields Each recommendation category surfaces relevant metadata fields during post creation — cuisine type and dish for restaurants, genre and streaming platform for movies, author and genre for books, region and vintage for wine, skin type and use-case for beauty products, etc. For restaurant recommendations, Recz also surfaces a holistic view of the Google Places entity by aggregating reviews from Google, Yelp, and other major review portals — giving the viewer both social trust and external credibility in one card.
Vibe Tags User-applied (and AI-suggested) descriptors that communicate the emotional and contextual quality of a recommendation — Cozy, Romantic, Lively, Hidden Gem, Overrated, etc.
"Recz'd It" CTA The primary engagement verb of the app. When a user sees someone else's post and wants to endorse it from their own perspective, they "Recz'd It" — adding social weight to that recommendation within the trust graph.
"I've Tried This" A lightweight engagement action that signals the user has experienced the recommendation, contributing to social proof without requiring a full post.
"Want to Try" A save mechanic that also signals intent, allowing Rexy and the system to follow up with "Try-It Moment" prompts.
Post Sharing & Cross-Posting Share recommendations internally (DMs, Group Chats) or externally (Instagram, WhatsApp, etc.).
 
4. RECZ LISTS
Recz List Creation Curated collections of recommendations grouped by theme, occasion, or category — e.g., "Best Ramen in Chicago," "Movies That Changed My Life," "Hidden Beaches in Goa." Lists are the primary long-form curation artifact on the app.
List Following & Discovery Users can follow lists from friends, Superstars, Recz Insiders, and brands. Lists appear in discovery feeds.
Sponsored & Celebrity Lists Recz Insiders have sponsored or promoted lists based on their location and persona — a Chicago-based foodie Insider might maintain a rotating "Best New Openings" list, while a wellness-focused Insider curates "Clean Eating Staples." Celebrities and verified Superstars can publish their own lists — a basketball player might share their sports recovery drinks, training spots, and gear; a Hollywood actor might maintain their favorite dining destinations and travel hideaways. These lists are followable, discoverable, and carry the aspirational weight of the lister's identity.
Collaborative Lists Multiple users can contribute to and co-edit a shared list — e.g., team trip planning, group watchlists.
 
5. RECZ ITINERARIES
Itinerary Builder A structured multi-stop, multi-day travel or experience plan built from Recz recommendations. Combines restaurants, activities, stays, and cultural experiences into a sequenced journey with timing and context.
AI-Assisted Itinerary Generation Rexy can draft itineraries from a prompt — "plan a 3-day trip to Tokyo for two foodies" — drawing from the social graph and external data.
Celebrity & Superstar Itineraries Verified Superstars and celebrities can publish their own itineraries that followers can adopt, remix, or book directly — "Travel like [Superstar]" becomes a discoverable and aspirational content format on the platform.
Itinerary Sharing & Social Proof Completed itineraries can be posted to the feed, shared with Buddies, or saved to a Recz List — turning travel and experience planning into social content that feeds back into the recommendation graph.
 
6. ROLLZ
Rollz Post Creation Short-form video and photo carousel posts — Instagram Reels-style — for sharing experiential content. Rollz are about showing, not just telling. A restaurant ambiance video, a travel highlight reel, a try-it moment.
Rollz Feed A vertical swipeable feed surfacing Rollz from friends, Superstars, and Recz Insiders. Discovery-first format.
Rollz Tagging & Music Location tags, Buddy tags, music overlays, and category tags within Rollz.
 
7. CHAINS / THREADS
Chain / Thread Post A text-forward, discussion-style post not tied to a recommendation. Users post takes, questions, polls, opinions on any topic — "Best city for nightlife?" or "Is Letterboxd better than Recz for movies?" — and others react, reply, and build on it.
Thread Engagement Nested replies, emoji reactions, and share/forward mechanics within a thread.
 
8. SOCIAL FEED
Home Social Feed A chronological or ranked feed of posts from people the user follows — friends, Superstars, Recz Insiders. The trust-filtered social timeline.
Engagement Layer Like, comment, save, share, and "Recz'd It" across all content types in the feed.
 
9. FOR YOU SCREEN
Personalized Dashboard A dynamic, Netflix/Spotify-style home screen organized into horizontally scrollable thematic rows. Not a raw timeline — a curated daily digest. Rows are context-aware: time of day, day of week, user mood, upcoming events.
Dynamic Row Types Rows include: Today's Highlights (birthdays, events, reminders), Trending in Your Circle, What Your Buddies Are Eating This Week, Weekend Getaways, Hidden Gems Near You, Watch Tonight, Recently Saved — revisit, and more. The row configuration changes based on context.
Time-of-Day Intelligence Morning rows differ from late-night rows. Friday afternoon content differs from Monday morning content. The 4U screen adapts to the user's life rhythm.
 
10. EXPLORE & SEARCH
Natural Language Search Full conversational search across all content types — users, posts, restaurants, movies, lists, events.
Category Browsing Structured browse by content type: Restaurants, Movies, Travel, Books, Music, Shopping, Wellness, etc.
Advanced Filters Filter by budget, vibe, occasion, cuisine, dietary restriction, distance, hours, and more.
Search by Chef / Dish Dedicated search flows for specific dish-level and chef-level restaurant discovery with social proof layered in.
Trending Discovery Surfaces trending content, posts, Rollz, and lists both globally and within the user's circle.
Hidden Gems Algorithmically surfaced underrated recommendations with high social credibility but low mainstream exposure.
Superstar Recommendations A dedicated discovery surface for content from verified Superstars and high-trust influencers.
 
11. REXY (AI CONCIERGE)
Rexy Chat Interface A conversational AI interface accessible via a floating button from any screen. The primary AI interaction surface on Recz. Natural language, multi-turn, contextually aware.
Intent-Aware Query Processing Rexy parses not just keywords but purpose, occasion, group size, budget, and emotional context. "Where should I take my parents for dinner" is understood as a calm, mid-range, family dining query.
Trust-Weighted Recommendations Results are ranked by social graph proximity — what your friends, Buddies, and Superstars have recommended carries more weight than anonymous reviews.
Explainable AI ("Why This Rec?") Every Rexy recommendation surfaces the reasoning — who recommended it, what vibe it matches, what the social proof is.
Celebrity Spotted Intelligence When a user asks Rexy about a place, Rexy surfaces celebrity sighting data as part of the recommendation context — "Tom Cruise was spotted here last month." Users can query directly: "Which restaurant did Kate Winslet go to?", "What does Taylor Swift drink?", "Where has Tom Cruise traveled?" — and Rexy cross-references celebrity affinity with the place being inquired about, adding an aspirational social layer to discovery.
Recz Extract on Posts Tap the Rexy icon on any post to get instant vibe analysis, social proof, pricing context, and action buttons — without leaving the feed.
Vibe Scanner Upload or snap a photo (of a restaurant, a room, a mood board) and Rexy deconstructs the vibe — mood tags, ambiance analysis, noise level, aesthetic — and surfaces matching places nearby.
Buddy Filter Lens Filter any set of Rexy results through a specific friend's taste profile. "Show me what Ananya would love here."
Spontaneous Mode ("I'm Bored") One-tap instant recommendation surfacing for users who don't want to type a query. Contextually weighted by time, location, and past behavior.
Rexy Persona System Users can customize Rexy's tone and personality — Chill, Sassy, Professional, Playful, Nerdy, Elegant. Rexy adapts its communication style accordingly.
Instant Collab Mode Turn a Rexy recommendation into a group plan — poll friends on options, schedule, and invite — all from within the conversation.
Train Your Rexy (Feedback Loop) Quick feedback chips after every recommendation (Loved it, Too expensive, Too far, Not my vibe) that tune Rexy's future outputs. Persistent personalization layer.
Try-It Moment Prompts After a user visits a Rexy-recommended place, the system prompts them to share the experience, closing the discovery-to-action loop.
Rexy as Travel Concierge Extended Rexy capability for travel — itinerary drafting, hotel suggestions, dining planning, activity sequencing across multiple days and cities.
Rexy for Food Delivery Rexy overlays on delivery marketplaces to guide dish-level ordering based on social graph intelligence, not just ratings.
Rexy Multi-Category Responses Beyond restaurants: Rexy answers for movies, TV shows, music, podcasts, books, travel, and wellness — with honest fallbacks ("I'm still learning about this, but...") where coverage is thin.
 
12. RECZ INSIDERS
Recz Insider Personas (AI Buddy) Synthetically generated user profiles across 10 archetypes (Foodie, Traveler, Nightlife, Wellness, Tech, Fashion, Bookworm, Film Buff, Local Insider, Campus Trendsetter) × 5 persona variations × 20 launch cities = 1,000 seeded Recz Insiders. They exist to solve cold start and populate the social graph.
Recz Insider Content Generation Recz Insiders post recommendations, create Recz Lists, post Rollz and Try-It Moments, and engage with user content (likes, comments, reactions). Content is hyper-localized to city, neighborhood, and university.
Recz Insider DM Conversations Recz Insiders respond to direct messages in tone-matched, persona-consistent conversations. They provide recommendations, share lists, and behave like real users within stated transparency limits.
Recz Insider Transparency Recz Insiders are labeled as "Recz Insider" or "Powered by Rexy" — semi-transparent, never fully hidden, but designed to feel social rather than robotic.
Localized Campus Recz Insiders University-specific Recz Insiders seeded to campuses in launch cities to drive early adoption in student communities.
 
13. BUDDY MATCHING & SOCIAL GRAPH
Buddy Discovery Surface users with compatible taste profiles for connection requests. The mechanism: not who you already know, but who you should know.
Buddy Matching A unique interface for discovering and connecting with taste-compatible people across categories (foodies, travelers, cinephiles, etc.).
Trust Graph The underlying architecture that weights every recommendation by social proximity, relationship depth, and shared taste history. Powers all personalization, Rexy, and For you.
Follow / Connect Standard social graph mechanics — follow Superstars unilaterally, connect with Buddies bilaterally.
Buddy Profile View See a Buddy's taste profile, their lists, their Rollz, their Try-It Moments, and mutual connections at a glance.
 
14. DIRECT MESSAGING & GROUP CHAT
One-on-One DMs Personal messaging between connected users and between users and Recz Insiders.
Group Chat Multi-user conversations for planning, sharing recommendations, and coordinating plans.
In-Chat Content Sharing Share posts, lists, Rollz, events, and recommendations directly within DMs and Group Chats.
Emoji Reactions & Quick Replies Lightweight engagement within conversations.
 
15. PLANS & EVENTS
Plan Creation Users can create a social plan around a recommendation — "Dinner at X on Friday, who's in?" — from any recommendation card or Rexy suggestion.
Event Hosting (Meetup-Style) Create and host structured events — meetups, group activities, pop-up gatherings — with title, description, date/time, location, and RSVP management.

Birthday Action Flow From the banner, the user can send a message, send a personalized recommendation, or combine a recommendation with a gift (booking, ticket, product).

Rexy Birthday Shortcut A conversational Rexy flow triggered on birthdays: "Help me send Sarah a birthday rec" — Rexy handles the entire flow including booking confirmation.
Upcoming Birthday Nudges Proactive push notifications 7 days and 1 day before a connection's birthday, with suggested recs based on the friend's taste profile.
 
17. TRY-IT MOMENTS

Badges & Achievements Earned through specific behaviors — posting across categories, consistent activity, high-engagement content. Badge system feeds identity and social proof.
AskRecz Boost At certain point thresholds, users unlock the ability to boost their recommendations for increased visibility within their network and on the explore feed.

Cross-Posting Users can cross-post Recz recommendations and content to Instagram and TikTok directly from the app, allowing influencers to maintain their existing audience while building their Recz presence simultaneously.

25. WELLNESS RECOMMENDATIONS
Wellness is a recommendation category on Recz like any other — covering health products, supplements, fitness gear, clean beauty, and lifestyle products. The difference from other categories lies in the discovery mechanic. Unlike restaurants (location-based) or movies (title search), physical wellness products are best discovered through barcode scanning — which becomes the native entry point for this category.
Product Barcode Scanning Scan a physical product barcode — packaged food, supplement, beauty product, or household item — to instantly pull up that product's Recz card. The scan triggers the same social discovery logic as any other Recz recommendation: what do people in your trusted network think of this?
Wellness Product Card The post-scan card shows product name, ingredient breakdown, nutritional data, and — most importantly — what your network has said about it. The format mirrors a restaurant or movie card: social proof first, data second.
Recz Wellness Score A composite score blending objective nutritional data (60%) with the trust-weighted social layer (40%). Consistent with how Recz weights all recommendations — trust from your circle matters more than anonymous aggregate ratings.
Wellness Feed Discovery Products scanned, recommended, and saved by people in your network surface in the Wellness category of your discovery feed — no different from how restaurant or book recommendations flow through the social graph.

Age Gating Content restrictions and UI filtering based on verified date of birth. Ensures compliance for alcohol, nightlife, and adult-targeted categories.
Child Safety Compliance COPPA-aligned controls for under-13 users, parental consent flows, and age-suspicious pattern detection.
Privacy Controls User-controlled profile visibility, post privacy settings, location sharing permissions, and data consent management.
 
27. NOTIFICATIONS & NUDGES
Contextual Push Notifications Birthday reminders, event nudges, Try-It Moment prompts, friend activity alerts, trending content alerts.
Rexy Proactive Suggestions Time- and location-aware push prompts from Rexy — "It's Friday night in Chicago, here's what's trending with your Buddies."
Booking Reminders Pre-event and day-of reminders for booked tables, tickets, and travel.
 
28. UNDERLYING INFRASTRUCTURE (PRODUCT-FACING)
Knowledge Graph The structured data layer connecting users, places, posts, social connections, and taste vectors. The backbone of all personalization and Rexy intelligence.
Multi-Agent AI Architecture The orchestration layer behind Rexy — multiple specialized agents (restaurant agent, travel agent, social agent, wellness agent, etc.) coordinated by a central LLM router.
Vibe Detection Engine (CLIP/BLIP-2) Image analysis layer that extracts mood, ambiance, aesthetic, and context from photos posted on the platform. Powers Vibe Scanner and vibe tag suggestions.
External API Integration Layer Connections to Yelp, Google Places, OpenTable, Zomato, Resy, DoorDash, Open Food Facts, Nutritionix, and streaming platforms to enrich Recz's knowledge with real-world data.
Search Engine (Typesense/Meilisearch) Fast, typo-tolerant, faceted search infrastructure powering all in-app search across users, places, posts, and lists.
