# Recz Complete Feature Checklist

## Core Discovery & Social Features

### Social Content & Engagement

- [ ] Create and share posts (restaurants, movies, shows, books, travel, shopping, experiences)
- [ ] Like, comment, and share posts from Buddies and Superstars
- [ ] View personalized home feed with recommendations from trusted circle
- [ ] Post images, captions, tags, music/effects and location data
- [ ] Cross-post to other social media platforms (Instagram, Facebook, etc.)
- [ ] Save posts and recommendations for later
- [ ] Explore feed for discovering new content
- [ ] Create text-based threads/chains on any topic (general discussions, non-recommendations)
- [ ] Rollz - Post short-form videos and photo carousels (Instagram Reels-style)
- [ ] Swipeable vertical feed for Rollz content

## Rexy Intelligence Features

- [ ] Query history tracking
- [ ] Category switching (restaurants, movies, travel, books, music)
- [ ] Recz Extract on Posts Tap Rexy on any post to get instant vibe analysis, pricing, tags, and Buddy insights
- [ ] Vibe Scanner Upload or snap photos to detect ambiance, mood tags, and get matching recommendations
- [ ] Buddy Filter Lens View recommendations through a specific friend's taste profile
- [ ] Spontaneous Mode ("I'm Bored") Get instant trending recommendations with minimal effort
- [ ] Rexy Persona System Customize Rexy's tone and personality (Chill, Sassy, Nerdy, Professional, Elegant, Playful)
- [ ] Instant Collab Mode Turn recommendations into plans with friends (polling, scheduling, voting)
- [ ] Feedback Learning Loop ("Train Your Rexy") Provide quick feedback to improve future recommendations
- [ ] Try-It Moments Share experiences after visiting Rexy-recommended places
- [ ] Social proof integration ("3 Buddies saved this")
- [ ] Graph-weighted personalization
- [ ] Intent parsing (date, group, solo, work, family occasions)
- [ ] Budget-aware filtering
- [ ] Location and proximity-based recommendations
- [ ] Explainable AI ("Why this rec?")
- [ ] Sentiment analysis from reviews
- [ ] Aggregated ratings from external sources (Google, Yelp, Zomato)
- [ ] AI-generated user profiles with personality archetypes
- [ ] AI Buddies create posts, lists, Rollz, and Try-It Moments
- [ ] AI Buddies like, comment, and engage with user content
- [ ] Respond to DMs in friendly, tone-matched conversations
- [ ] Provide personalized recommendations based on persona preferences
- [ ] Local, hyper-localized AI Buddies (university, city, neighborhood-specific)
- [ ] Multiple archetypes: Foodie, Traveler, Nightlife, Wellness, Tech, Fashion, etc.
- [ ] Semi-transparent AI labeling (tagged as "AI Buddy" or "Powered by Rexy")
