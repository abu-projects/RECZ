# RECZ — Screens That Need Adjustment
## Based on the 3 Core Pillars
1. Recommendation
2. Social
3. Taste Identity

---

# CORE PRODUCT DIRECTION

RECZ should not feel like:
- an AI recommendation app
- a Yelp clone
- a dating app

RECZ should feel like:
> A social taste network powered by trusted circles, shared vibes, and identity-driven discovery.

The app should communicate:
- WHO you trust
- WHAT your vibe is
- WHY this recommendation fits you

---

# IMPORTANT APPROVED PRODUCT DIRECTIONS

## ✅ Approved
- "Taste Circle" instead of Match/Buddy
- Mutual follow = enters your Taste Circle
- Following someone lets you discover their recommendations
- Communities naming approved
- Communities should feel interest/taste-based
- No dating language or dating feeling

- AI-assisted

---

## Rexy Flow

### Step 1
User starts naturally:
- "I want to recommend a place"

---

### Step 2
Rexy asks:
- Which place?
- Voice or text?

---

### Step 3
Place detection card:
- Do you mean this place?

---

### Step 4
Rexy drafts recommendation.

---

### Step 5
User refines:
- tags
- rating
- photos
- vibe
- timing

---

### Step 6
Post recommendation.

---
