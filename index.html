29. Help & About

How Recz Works

Lists

Curate lists of recommendations for any occasion — Readings, travel plans, or birthday celebrations. Share them with friends or make them collaborative.

Taste Match

See how much your taste aligns with friends. Your match score grows as you save, rate, and recommend similar things. High match = great suggestions!

Rexy AI

Your personal discovery assistant. Ask Rexy for recommendations based on time, mood, group size, budget, or any occasion. Powered by your taste + your circle.

Smart Personalization

Your feed adapts to time of day, season, friendships, and interests. Morning picks differ from evening suggestions. Weekend vibes are different from weekday shortcuts.

Friend Influence

Your friends' saves, likes, and recommendations directly shape what you see. The more aligned your taste, the stronger their influence on your feed.
